Horse riding

I have been doing horse riding for over 6 years now, although I wish I had done it much earlier! I have always loved animals and contact with them since I was little, so doing this activity is a no-brainer for me. I would advise everyone to start this activity, or to try it at least once in their life, as it can teach us a lot about empathy, perseverance and patience.

Traveling

Since I was very young and thanks to my parents who themselves traveled all over the world, I love this activity. The discovery of another culture, new landscapes, new people... Traveling is a way for me to break the routine and to feel free of my choices. I think that it is important for everyone to travel at least once in his life, whether it is in the same country or on the other side of the world!

Music

I love listening to music, as do many people. But most of all I love to feel like I'm in a movie or in another universe after each song. Music, even if I don't make it, is a way to escape but also to be creative. It is music that pushed me to start video editing, as I have many visual inspirations when I listen to music.
